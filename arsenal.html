History



Salesforce started life in March, 1999 contained out of a one bedroom apartment next to Marc Benioff’s house, atop Telegraph Hill, San Francisco. The three men that started working in this tiny office along with Marc? Parker Harris, Frank Dominguez and Dave Moellenhoff. Salesforce runs entirely in the cloud. This means that Salesforce is online, there is no software/hardware. No expensive setup costs, maintances and can work from any devices as long as it has an internet connection setup. Salesforce is a Customer Relationship Management (CRM), which is a strategy for managing all your company's relationships and interactions with your customers and potential customers. It helps you improve your profitability.

Salesforce has 3 releases each year, the most recent being Salesforce Winter'17 and soon Salesforce Spring'17. This constant upgrading has help make Salesforce (Sales Cloud) the world's largerst CRM System.
  • 2005- App Exchange:
    AppExchange apps are customized for Salesforce and extend Salesforce to every department and every industry. It's a proven ecosystem of apps with millions of installs and thousands of customer reviews to help you find the best match for your business.

  • 2006- Apex:
    An object oriented programmings language for Salesforce Developers.

  • 2007- VisualForce:
    Framework which allows developers to build customer user interfaces.

  • 2008- Force.com:
    Used the simplify the development and deployment of cloud based applications and websites.

  • 2009- Chatter:
    Connects every employee tofiles,charts and able to interact with other users in your organisation.

  • 2010- Database.com:
    Enables users to focus on building apps instead of managing the datavase.

  • 2013- Salesforce1:
    Allows a user to use Salesforce on their smart phone, so they are able to work on the move.

  • 2016- Lightning:
    Lightning includes the Lightning Component Framework and some exciting tools for developers. Lightning makes it easier to build responsive applications for any device.

  • Upcoming- Einstein
    Einstein is coming out in the Spring '17 release. Advanced AI capabilities into sales, service, marketing and more, empowering companies to deliver more personalized and predictive customer experiences
